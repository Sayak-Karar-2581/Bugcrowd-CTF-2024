The undercover

Buggy is on a secret mission, undercover as a tourist in this culturally significant vacation destination. As Buggy wanders the narrow alleys paved with cobblestones and lined with colourful buildings, the mission hinges on uncovering a hidden secret. Your mission is to discover the coordinates of this mysterious location.

Find the location (latitude, longitude) of this area rounded to 3 decimal places and wrap it in flag{}, e.g. flag{-19.242, 47.497}
